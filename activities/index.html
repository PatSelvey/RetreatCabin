Activities

We share over 85 acres of beautiful, rugged mountain property. There are several trails for hiking and soothing spring fed creek runs through the bottom of our property year around. There are varieties of wildlife to be seen as well. Wild turkey, squirrel and whitetail deer just to name a few can be seen daily if you keep an eye out for them.

Wilkesboro is just 7 minutes away and boasts a variety of nice family restaurants, shopping, entertainment, mountain crafts, emporiums, outlet shops, and great antique shopping.

Winston-Salem is about a 45 minute drive and features the Historic Old Salem district where you can explore the daily activities of Moravians who settled Salem in back in 1766.

Hickory is renowned as the “Furniture Capital of the World.” Blowing Rock and Boone offer arts, crafts, skiing and make sure you explore Grandfather Mountain. The whole family will enjoy digging for gems at Hiddenite’s Emerald Mine.

Kerr Scott Lake is only 10 minutes by car where you can ski, fish, swim, hike and let the kids play. Enjoy a cappuccino or a freshly baked pastry at Talia’s Espresso, a warm and inviting bistro-style cafe located in North Wilkesboro. And of course you can take a drive on the Scenic Blue Ridge Parkway where you find even more trails, waterfalls, fishing and beautiful landscapes posing for your camera.
