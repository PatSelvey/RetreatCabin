Hidden Hollow Retreat Cabin

In the heart of the Brushy Mountains, deep in the woodlands surrounding Moravian Falls, there nestles a snug, secluded, yet modern haven for those seeking a quiet place for retreat and meditation.

The Hidden Hollow Retreat Cabin has been a vision in the making for the past seven years by master carpenter, and former pastor, Pat Selvey. The purpose of the Center is to provide Christians an affordable haven where personal ministry, retreat, renewed vision and quiet meditation would be the agenda of the day.

With every joint and tenon lovingly fashioned by hand, the Hidden Hollow Retreat Cabin boasts a rustic, yet thoroughly modern feel that allows for comfortable relaxation far from the cares of the world.

Located approximately seven miles from the town of Wilkesboro, North Carolina, there is fishing by the hour or day at a stocked pond nearby, and the wildlife as well as the breathtaking sunsets can be viewed from the cabin’s decks. Hiking and walking along the creek are also easily accessible. There are many restaurants, grocery stores and other shopping in nearby Wilkesboro.

However our most coveted attraction is the Brushy Mountain Range. There are mountain range views all around our property. The sun gloriously rises each morning off the front porch and graciously sets off the rear decks. We are truly blessed to have such a place and are happy to share it with other believers.
